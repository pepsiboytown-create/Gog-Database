About GOGs

GOGs are ancient cosmic beings that accidentally discovered tacos and then taught whole civilisations how to moonwalk. They've won more awards than there are stars, including the prestigious "Golden Pebble of Honor" (won 27 times). Every year a GOG climbs the highest cloud to perform the Ritual of Plenty — this is how seasons are invented.

Recent accomplishments:

  • Built the first intergalactic pogo stick factory.
  • Invented vertical rain (it falls up on Tuesdays).
  • Hired three thousand squirrels as official taste-testers.
