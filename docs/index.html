About GOGs

GOGs are ancient cosmic beings that accidentally discovered tacos and then taught whole civilisations how to moonwalk. They've won more awards than there are stars, including the prestigious "Golden Pebble of Honor" (won 27 times). Every year a GOG climbs the highest cloud to perform the Ritual of Plenty — this is how seasons are invented.

GOG Creators

The visionaries behind the chaos, meet the brilliant minds (and one moderately-skilled hamster) responsible for GOGs:

🎨 ZigbyTT

Title: The Idea Person & Concept Artist

Specialty: Early prototype GOG drawings, wild ideas, creative direction

Known For: Coming up with the most chaotic and hilarious GOG concepts. Sketches first drafts and wild proto-designs. Never afraid to push creative boundaries.

Payment Terms: Accepts payment in stickers, confetti, and creative compliments

Fun Fact: Once drew a GOG so weird that even Shadowh3art had to ask for clarification.

🌙 Shadowh3art

Title: The Finisher & Polish Master

Specialty: Making GOGs look clean and professional, final art direction

Known For: Taking ZigbyTT's wild sketches and turning them into gorgeous polished GOGs. Has impeccable taste and pixel-perfect precision. Makes everything look effortlessly cool.

Payment Terms: Prefers payment in clean aesthetics, smooth animations, and compliments about their art

Fun Fact: Can polish a GOG so well that it makes people question why they didn't think of it first.

🔥 Marble Helios

Title: The Prime Hater of GOGs

Specialty: Scheming endlessly to sabotage GOGs, master of anti-GOG tactics

Known For: Obsessively plotting against ZigbyTT’s creations, determined to unravel every polished GOG. His taste runs toward chaos, and his precision is in finding flaws others miss.

Payment Terms: Accepts rewards in ruined aesthetics, broken animations, and praise for his relentless vendettas

Fun Fact: Can spot a GOG from miles away and instantly devise a plan to make its life miserable.

Quote from both creators: "We have no idea how any of this works anymore. We just keep adding features and hope nobody notices." — ZigbyTT & Shadowh3art, circa Never
